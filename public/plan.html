Format guide

One section per day. Each exercise on its own line:

# Monday: Push Day
1. Bench Press | weight | Arms-Push | 4x8 | 80kg
2. Pull-ups | bodyweight | Arms-Pull | 3x10

# Tuesday: Leg Day
1. Squats | weight | Legs-Push | 4x6 | 100kg

Types: weight, bodyweight, assisted, cardio

Categories: Legs-Push, Legs-Pull, Arms-Push, Arms-Pull, Core-Push, Core-Pull

Weight is optional — omit for bodyweight/cardio exercises.

Only days present in the text are updated. Workout history is never affected.
